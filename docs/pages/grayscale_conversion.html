1. Arithmetic mean¶

$$ \large \varsigma = \frac{\sum_{i}^{n} C_i}{n} = \frac{R+G+B}{3} $$
In [6]:
img_GS01 = np.sum(img_RGB, axis=2)/3
img_GS01 = img_GS01.astype(int)
In [7]:
histogram(img_GS01, bins=2**8)

2. Geometric mean¶

$$ \large \varsigma = \left(\prod_{i}^{n}C_i\right)^\frac{1}{n}=(R \cdot G \cdot B)^\frac{1}{3} $$
In [8]:
img_GS02 = np.prod(img_RGB, axis=2)**(1/3)
img_GS02 = img_GS02.astype(int)
In [9]:
histogram(img_GS02, bins=2**8)

3. Harmonic mean¶

$$ \large \varsigma = \frac{n}{\sum_{i}^{n}\frac{1}{C_i}}=\frac{3}{\frac{1}{R}+\frac{1}{G}+\frac{1}{B}} $$
In [10]:
img_GS03 = 3/np.sum(1/img_RGB, axis=2)
img_GS03 = img_GS03.astype(int)
In [11]:
histogram(img_GS03, bins=2**8)
